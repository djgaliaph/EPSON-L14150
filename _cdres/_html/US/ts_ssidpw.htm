Select the SSID of the 2.4 GHz band of your router and enter its password below.

Locate the SSID and password of the 2.4 GHz band on the router label or card, or in its manual. The password may be called a key.
Note: The SSID may include with a “g”, “G”, or “b/g/n”. The password may be called a network or Wi-Fi key, or a wireless password. The style varies by router manufacturer.
