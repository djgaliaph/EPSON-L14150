How To Display an SSID in Computer

1 . Open the selection screen of the SSID you want to connect to.

For Windows 10
Click on the taskbar.
The available SSIDs are displayed on the screen.
For Windows 8.1/Windows 8/Windows 7
Click on the taskbar.
The available SSIDs are displayed on the screen.
For Windows Vista
Right-click the wireless icon on the taskbar, and then click View available wireless networks.
The available SSIDs are displayed on the Connect to Wireless Network screen.
For Windows XP
Click Start > Control Panel > Network Connections > Create a new connection.
The available SSIDs are displayed on the Wireless Network Connection screen.
For MacOS
Click menu.
The available SSIDs are displayed on the screen.
