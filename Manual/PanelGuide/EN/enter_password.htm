Entering the Password and Connecting

1 . Connect to the SSID.

For Windows 10
Clear Connect automatically, and then click Connect.
For Windows 8.1/Windows 8/Windows 7/Windows Vista
Click Connect.
For Windows XP
Right-click the SSID, and then click Connect.
For MacOS
No action required.

2 . Connect the printer and computer by entering the password displayed on the printerâ€™s control panel into the computer.

For Windows XP, enter the Network key and the Confirm network key.

3 . For Windows 8.1/Windows 8/Windows 7/Windows Vista users, you can set your computer to connect to the printer automatically every time you turn on your computer.

You do not have to set this if you only want to connect to the printer when you use it.
